Welcome!

This is the official Point5 Podcast website. You'll find all the official links and information on this website.

This website is for guests and listeners. Both are strongly recommended to visit the community chat tab to get on the community server. There, you'll find the latest updates and resources.

Use the various contact options, if you don't find what you need.

Community

Guests and listeners can join our community spaces. Here, we can chat and get the notifications that are important to us.

Previous and upcoming guests can chat to the community in the server. General discussion is also available using message, voice, and video. Different communication channels have been made to provide different rooms. This provides a location for discussion and allows you to meet others.

Notifications are available for different needs. You can setup your notifications with our different channels to receive what you want. This is available for guests and listeners.
